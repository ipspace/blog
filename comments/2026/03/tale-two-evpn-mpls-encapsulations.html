    You can tell Arista EOS not to add a control word:

    ~~~text switch(config)#mpls evpn switch(config-mpls-evpn)#no label-stack control-word switch(config-mpls-evpn)#exit ~~~

    See https://www.arista.com/en/support/toi/eos-4-21-3f/14125-configurable-l2-evpn-mpls-control-word (accessing Arista's so called TOIs, the actually important/interesting part of Arista's docs requires customer status)
