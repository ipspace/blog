    Great Post. We at CPLANE NETWORKS have been touting the benefits of multi-protocol centralized service orchestration for a long time. Whether it's bandwidth management, path computation or "service stitching" for example, provisioning vxlan overlays and mpls vpn with path/quality control together to provide a higher service offering, the need is to understand each device and it's capabilities in an overall system. For a long time OpenFlow and control plane have taken most of the oxygen out of the room. The key of successful orchestration is being able to straddle newer and older technologies. Service Orchestration by nature is never green field.
