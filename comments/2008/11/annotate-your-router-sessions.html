    That exclamation mark is useful in one other subtle little way. If you've typed out a really long command and just realized that you need to go enable something else first, you can CNTL-A and add in the exclamation mark. Hit return and go do what's in need of doing. Then you can up arrow back and do another CNTL-A to remove the exclamation.
