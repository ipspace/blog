  2. Ivan Pepelnjak 21 March 2011 20:00
    This is not an HP thing. If you have static LAG and a member link fails in non-obvious way (forwarding loss with no carrier loss), you'll get a traffic black hole. LACP has a keepalive mechanism and thus detects that one of the links in LAG has failed and removes it from LAG.
