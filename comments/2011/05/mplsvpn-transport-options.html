  2. Ivan Pepelnjak 26 May 2011 20:47
    Focusing just on transport - the main reasons to choose IP(VPN)-MPLS(VPN)-MPLS(LDP) over IP(VPN)-MPLS(VPN)-IP would be overhead and frame size. You can squeeze labeled frame in a baby-jumbo (not relevant in the DC).

    It's probably also easier/faster to label switch in hardware than to do full IP lookup (although I don't think the difference is relevant these days).

    Last but not least, if you use MPLS labeling, you'll be able to interwork with almost any existing MPLS/VPN gear; some boxes don't support MPLS-over-mGRE (Cat6500, for example).
  3. Ivan Pepelnjak 26 May 2011 20:49
    One more (also doesn't apply to DC, at least not to the way it's implemented today) - Fast Reroute works on labeled traffic, not sure if it works on pure IP traffic (or at least it might be implementation/platform/vendor dependent).
