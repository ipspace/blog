    I just recently implemented QoS on my MPLS WAN. It is a beautiful thing.

    I have four queues set up.

    Queue 1 - Priority voice queue (equals 20% of the link)
    Queue 2 - Mission Critical Traffic (equals 25%)
    Queue 3 - Transactional Traffic (equals 20%)
    Queue 4 - Default Queue or Everything Else (10%)

    It really depends on your business needs as what goes in which queue. But my links can reach 90% utilization(large downloads) but the site doesn't notice any performance impact because as soon as they need something business critical policing will kick in. QoS will make a happy network engineer.
