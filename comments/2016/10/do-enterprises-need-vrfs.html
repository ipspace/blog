    We have three different zones. External datacenter networks, internal datacenter networks, office networks. Some sites have all three of them. We use different VRFs for that, exactly because we don't want to use stretched VLANs, and use RIP on VMs to announce their location for VM mobility. And firewalls with interfaces in all three VRFs for inter-VRF traffic.

    Can't say it's simple but it works with less headaches than one routing domain.
