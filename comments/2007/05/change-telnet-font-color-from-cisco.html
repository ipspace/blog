    Hi there, I found I also had to set the international flag on the VTYs before I could make this work:

    R1# conf t
    Enter configuration commands, one per line. End with CNTL/Z.
    R1(config)# line vty 0 15
    R1(config-line)# international
    R1(config-line)# end
    R1# wr

    Another note - if you just want a colourful banner, use [0m to reset your changes.
