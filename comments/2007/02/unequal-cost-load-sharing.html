  2. Ivan Pepelnjak 13 February 2007 16:56
    CEF (or any other load-sharing mechanism) does not consider interface bandwidth (the only exception being MPLS TE tunnels). To perform non-equal load split you need a different approach, for example, creative use of static routes.
