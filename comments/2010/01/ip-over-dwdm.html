    You might want to check out CWDM. Much cheaper to start up, and (and this is major) pure passive equipment options on short distance (short distance : theoretically 20km, practically 40km, and you might get lucky at 50km, with the new sfp's that should go on sale this year that's supposed to get closer to 100 km).
