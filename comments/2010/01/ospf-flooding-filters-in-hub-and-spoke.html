  5. Jose Jara 18 March 2013 18:18
    An alternative, for example if we are using an IOS that do not support this feature or we are using another vendor, could be to MaxAge all the LSA's from the Hub increasing the transmit delay to 3600 seconds. However, the adjacency will be broken due to "too many retransmissions" , to avoid that we should increase the retransmit interval to a high value in order to maintain the adjacency up.

    So, the configuration in the Hub would be:

    interface
    ip ospf retransmit-interval 6000
    ip ospf transmit-delay 3600
