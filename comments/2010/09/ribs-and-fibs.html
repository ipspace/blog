    Next-hop of a BGP or static route could be reachable by more than one IGP path (several equal-cost OSPF/EIGRP paths to the same destination). In that case, you'd see one BGP or static route entry in the RIB and multiple entries in FIB (at least that's what you'd have seen before CEF switching rewrite, not sure what the latest IOS releases would show).
