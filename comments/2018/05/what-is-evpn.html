    EVPN was actually conceived as a DC protocol. It was standardized in L2VPN WG because there was no DC IETF WG at the time. It's a good replacement for VPLS because, aside from integration with TE, WAN overlay requirements are mostly a subset of DC overlay requirements.

      Running them on top of EVPN is thus easier to set up.
