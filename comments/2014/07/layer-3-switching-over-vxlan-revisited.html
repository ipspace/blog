  2. Bruno Rijsman 21 July 2014 14:09
    @darkfaber: VXLAN was introduced because it supports tunneling over IP Fabrics and because it has good support for multipath (entropy in UDP source port). S-VLAN and QinQ cannot tunnel over an IP Fabric; they only support Ethernet Fabrics. IP Fabrics are more scalable than Ethernet Fabrics.
