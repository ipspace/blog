  3. Aaron 23 October 2007 20:56
    I just wanted to note that the quiet-mode access-class overrides your VTY access-class for the duration of quiet-mode. So, if you have a ACL applied to the VTY already and the router goes into quiet-mode, the quiet-mode access-class is used in place of your VTY ACL. When quiet-mode is over, your VTY ACL is re-applied.
