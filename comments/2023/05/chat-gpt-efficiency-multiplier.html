    I think it can be summarized as "ChatGPT provides the content whereas we as humans need to put that in the right context" It's one of things I am trying to achieve when dealing with lots of engineers working for different customers. It's at that inflection point where what you know meets the why. ChatGPT can help with the what question but can never replace the why. That's where skill, experience and attitude come in.
