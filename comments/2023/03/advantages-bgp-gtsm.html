    Since I have just fallen into this trap: the Cisco IOS-XE BGP implementation does not support GTSM (called *TTL Security Check for BGP Neighbor Sessions*) for IBGP, it is only available for EBGP sessions.

    So while the mechanism in general would work for IBGP, not all implementations support this.
