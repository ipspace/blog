    Another option would be to configure a copy of the network connectivity + services (VLANs, IP ranges, VRFs, firewall instances/contexts, load balancer instances/contexts) at the DR site, and have it isolated at the point where it would connect to the outside.     That way all you need in case of a DR event is to swing the external connectivity.
