    I would argue that the "defining the problem" is not a technical discussion. Rather, the need for software defined network overlays (NSX) is driven by the overhead of current network implementation and orchestration. Compute and virtualization teams are now demanding a network that keeps pace with compute virtualization.

    As physical compute and storage have been commoditized, datacenter networking will be soon.
