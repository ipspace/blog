  4. Ivan Pepelnjak 30 November 2010 12:08
    Communities were never intended to have global reach (and most of them have local significance anyway), so it's not necessarily a bad idea that they're filtered by default.

    BTW, the RFC logic is not violated. Communities are optional transitive attributes, which means you should pass them on IF YOU DON'T KNOW WHAT TO DO WITH THEM. If you do, you're free to do whatever you wish.
