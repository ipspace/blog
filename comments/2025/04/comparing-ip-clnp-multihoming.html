    We have a generic site multi-homing solution: the LISP protocol. Now it is a full Internet proposed standard. I know it is not popular besides Cisco, but from the technology perspective it is a quite good solution.

    As a plus benefit, you get a true multilink mobility support without any additional stuff. Even PMIPv6 cannot do that kind of mobility, since it is only good for single link mobility.

    And then you can combine mutlink mobility with site multihoming. Perfect solution for safety critical and mission critical networks.
