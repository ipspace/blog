--- url: /2007/12/ospf-remote-lab-exercises.html title: "OSPF Remote Lab Exercises" date: "2007-12-21T07:22:00.001+01:00" tags: [ OSPF,labs,Training ] --- Have you ever wanted to practice with all the aspects of the OSPF technology, from simple single-area scenarios to a complex MPLS environment? The new remote lab product we've just released gives you the opportunity to test a number of OSPF concepts and configuration techniques. The Open Shortest Path First - Complete Technology lab bundle is a collection of exercises taken from standard Cisco courses (BSCI, MPLS and IP6FD) enhanced by specific OSPF scenarios (Non-Broadcast Multi-Access, Sham link support) and complete real-life deployment scenario (OSPF Superlab), enabling you to gain advanced skills in configuring and monitoring OSPF in complex and diverse network environments.
