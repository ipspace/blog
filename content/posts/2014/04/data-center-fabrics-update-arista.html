It’s time for the next Data Center Fabrics Update session, and so I spent the whole Friday poring over Arista’s data sheets and EOS documentation. As expected, they launched numerous switches in the last year, and added a bunch of new features to EOS. I'll explain some of those in more details:

  • LACP fallback;
  • OpenStack integration;
  • Monitor mode in OpenFlow;
  • DirectFlow (OpenFlow functionality configured through REST API);
  • IP-in-IP tunneling (head-end for direct-server-return load balancing)
