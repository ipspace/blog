--- url: /2014/10/interop-new-york-it-was-great-fun/ title: "Interop New York: It Was Great Fun" date: "2014-10-05T17:42:00.000+02:00" ---

Last week’s Interop New York was hard work (three workshops in two days), but also lots of nerdy fun. I love doing workshops with smart participants who bring their real-life problems to the room and challenge my assumptions and conclusions, and I had plenty of these interactions during the week. Thank you all (you know who you are)!

The other great part of Interop is meeting my Networking Tech Field Day friends. These chats usually turn into amazing conversations that help me fit together bits and pieces I’ve been struggling with for weeks or months, often resulting in (hopefully) interesting blog posts

Finally, a huge Thank You to the Interop team for a fantastically well-organized conference, and I hope to see you all in Las Vegas in a few months.
