It’s always fun watching people replacing an older IBM mainframe with a new model – they usually replace a hall-full of gear with a fridge-like box. Something similar is happening in the private cloud world – you can build most private clouds with a handful of high-end servers and two top-of-rack switches.

We’ll discuss the implications of incredible advances in server and switching technologies in the new Designing a Private Cloud webinar (register here). The initial materials have already been uploaded – browse through them after registering and send me any additional topics you’d like to see discussed.
