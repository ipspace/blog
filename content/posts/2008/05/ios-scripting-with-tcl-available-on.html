--- url: /2008/05/ios-scripting-with-tcl-available-on.html title: "IOS scripting with Tcl available on almost all platforms" date: "2008-05-05T08:19:00.001+02:00" tags: [ Tcl,command line interface ] --- The Tcl scripting (invoked with tclsh command) is now available on almost all IOS-based platforms (apart from the low-end Catalyst switches). For high-end distributed platforms, use the 12.2(33)SRC release, for Catalyst 6500 the 12.2(33)SXH release. On most other platforms, you can use mainstream 12.4 release.
You can find more Tclsh-related information in the Tclsh on Cisco IOS tutorial. Sample Tclsh scripts are available in the Tclsh script library. If you need expert help in planning, developing or deploying Tclsh scripts in your network, contact the author.
