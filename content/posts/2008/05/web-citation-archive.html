--- url: /2008/05/web-citation-archive.html title: "Web Citation Archive" date: "2008-05-05T20:19:00.000+02:00" --- The blogging world is an interestingly dynamic place ... sometimes a page would vanish moments after you would mention it in a blog post :) Google Cache might be a short term solution, but you could use a much better tool: Webcite gives you the ability to store a snapshot of a web page at the time you've cited it.
