Starting with a short message to anyone interested in our on-site events in Switzerland: on March 10th we’re running our first 2020 workshop, focusing on Docker and containers.

I totally reworked the material, adding tons of new Docker networking examples (including deep dive into iptables) and a few fun things like building an Ansible container, or starting the whole NetBox stack with a single command. Even if you don’t plan to deploy containers in your production network, you might drop by just for that part.

And now for the upcoming webinars:

  • On March 5th, David Peñaloza Seijas plans to dive deeper in Cisco SDWAN. The content of the first webinar is already online and available with free or paid subscription.
  • I’ll run a new version of Upcoming Internet Challenges webinar on March 19th focusing on whether anything has changed and/or improved in the almost ten years since I did the last session on the topic (yeah, I’ve been doing webinars for over a decade).
  • Lukas Krattiger and Mark Nowell will talk about 400 GbE in data center and WAN environments on March 26th.

Finally, the Networking in Public Cloud Deployments online course continues on March 3rd with automation session with Ned Bellavance, and with a storage-focused session with Howard Marks on March 17th. You can’t register for the live course anymore, but you can get all the materials, support, hands-on exercises, exercise review… with the Expert Subscription.
