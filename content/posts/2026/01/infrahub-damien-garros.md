Why do we need [Infrahub](https://github.com/opsmill/infrahub), another network automation tool? What does it bring to the table, who should be using it, and why is it using a graph database internally?

I discussed these questions with [Damien Garros](https://www.linkedin.com/in/damiengarros/), the driving force behind Infrahub, the founder of [OpsMill](https://opsmill.com/) (the company developing it), and a [speaker](https://www.ipspace.net/Author:Damien_Garros) in the [ipSpace.net Network Automation course](https://www.ipspace.net/Building_Network_Automation_Solutions).
