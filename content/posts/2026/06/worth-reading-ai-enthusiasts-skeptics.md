Charity Majors wrote an excellent article describing [AI enthusiasts in a race against time and AI skeptics in a race against entropy](https://charitydotwtf.substack.com/p/ai-enthusiasts-are-in-a-race-against). Fair warning: its very first sentence triggered an acute case of PTSD:

> I recently attended a talk where one of the presenters made some pretty…astonishing claims about what they had achieved by the pure, uncut power of vibe coding.

I've seen way too many presentations making "astonishing claims" about the unlimited unicorn-driven powers of OpenFlow, SDN, OpenDaylight, or Ansible.

Hope you'll enjoy the rest of the article.
