_netlab_ release 26.03 is out. Here are the highlights:

- [New Cisco IOS XR features](https://netlab.tools/release/26.03/#release-26-03-ios-xr): SR-MPLS, MPLS/VPN, MPLS 6PE, EVPN over MPLS, VRRP, BGP session parameters, routing policies, prefix filters, AS-path filters, community filters, and static routes
- [Juniper cRPD](https://netlab.tools/caveats/#caveats-crpd) support (mostly by [@leec-666](https://github.com/leec-666))
- [EVPN/VXLAN](https://netlab.tools/module/evpn/#module-evpn) on Juniper vPTX and vJunos-router by [@rickycraft](https://github.com/rickycraft)
- OpenBSD BGP support by [@remilocherer](https://github.com/remilocherer)
- [SR-MPLS support for OSPFv2](https://netlab.tools/module/sr-mpls/#sr-mpls-platforms) on EOS, FRR, IOS XE, IOS XR
- EVPN/MPLS improvements, including [using SR-MPLS transport](https://netlab.tools/module/evpn/#global-evpn-parameters)

For even more details, check the [release notes](https://netlab.tools/release/26.03/).
