I listened to Ethan Banks’ presentation on lessons learned running active-active data centers years ago at Interop, and liked it so much that I asked him to talk about the same topic during the Building Next-Generation Data Center course.

Not surprisingly, Ethan did a stellar job, and when I heard he was working on QoS part of an upcoming book asked him whether he’d be willing to do a webinar on QoS. Took us a long while to set everything up, but we’re finally there: Ethan will run a QoS Fundamentals webinar on November 9th.

Other events in the new few weeks

  • Data center automation event and deep-dive workshop in Zurich (Switzerland, not Montana) on November 14th;
  • Third session of Network Visibility with Flow Data covering flow analysis on January 23rd.
